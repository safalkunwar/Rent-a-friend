# SATHI Help & Support Center

Welcome to the SATHI Support Center. SATHI is Nepal's premier Companion Guide and Local Experience platform. We are committed to helping you build trusted, safe, and memorable guided experiences in Nepal.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. What is SATHI?
SATHI is a marketplace connecting verified local companions ("Sathis") with travelers and residents seeking guided tours, local insights, language translation, or shared experiences in Nepal. SATHI is strictly a professional companionship and guiding platform.

### 2. Is SATHI a dating application?
**No.** SATHI is strictly **NOT** a dating platform. All companions are verified professional guides, language specialists, or local experts. Any behavior violating this policy will result in immediate account suspension and blacklisting.

### 3. How do I book a Companion?
1. Find a companion on the **Explore** or **Search** page.
2. View their profile, reviews, and hourly rates (in NPR).
3. Click **Book Sathi** and enter your desired date, time, duration, and meeting point.
4. Select a payment option (Khalti or eSewa).
5. Once the companion reviews and accepts the booking, the status will update to "Accepted" and real-time chat will unlock.

### 4. How does the messaging system work?
- Before booking, you have up to **2 free inquiries** to ask basic questions.
- Once a booking is **Accepted** by the companion, full real-time messaging is permanently unlocked for that companion during the booking window.

### 5. What is the SOS Safety Button?
The SOS Safety Button is a trust and safety feature. It is automatically enabled only during an active, accepted booking session. If you feel unsafe or experience an emergency, clicking the SOS button will instantly:
- Dispatch a high-priority alert to SATHI Admin.
- Notify your assigned companion.
- Log your GPS coordinates and booking details in our secure database for emergency dispatch.
